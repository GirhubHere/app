OUR PURPOSE

We connect the farmers of Ukraine's black-soil heartland to consumers worldwide — delivering essential food, feed and fuel crops, safely and sustainably.

OUR STORY

A Force Multiplier for Ukrainian Farmers

Five Agra Select was built on a simple conviction: the world's finest black soil deserves a world-class route to market. We work directly with producers — no long broker chains — supporting their harvest and connecting it to buyers who demand verified quality.

  • Direct contracts with trusted farms across the chernozem regions
  • Pre-harvest field monitoring and origin traceability for every lot
  • Independent surveyor inspection (SGS / Control Union) at loading
  • GAFTA / FOSFA standard contracts with transparent terms
